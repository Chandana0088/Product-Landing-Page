Starbucks is a multinational coffee company and coffeehouse chain based in Seattle, Washington. It was founded in 1971 and offers a variety of coffee, tea, and other beverages, as well as pastries, snacks, and other food items. With over 30,000 locations worldwide, Starbucks is one of the largest coffee chains in the world.
